Vikram (2022)

Vikram is a Tamil action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Kamal Haasan in a commanding role as a retired agent drawn back into a covert mission. The story revolves around a secret task force dismantling a powerful drug syndicate, with intense action sequences and layered storytelling. The film is part of the Lokesh Cinematic Universe, connecting characters and plots from other movies like Kaithi. With standout performances by Vijay Sethupathi and Fahadh Faasil, Vikram blends mass appeal with smart writing, making it a landmark in modern Tamil cinema.

Cast Members

-->Kamal Haasan as Agent Vikram / Karnan

-->Vijay Sethupathi as Santhanam (drug syndicate leader)
